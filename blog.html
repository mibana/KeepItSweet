The Workload
Sept 23rd, 2015

The second week of school and I'm already stressing. I keep telling myself that it's only the beginning, but at the same time, I know it is a lot more work than usual. I think that the reason school seems very difficult is because the professors leave all the responsibilty up to you. It is my responsibility to keep up with readings, assignments, and quizzes, and it is my responsibility to make sure I understand. However, I believe things do get better with time, and it will definitely take some getting used to, but here and now is where I want to be.

Moving In
Sept 6th, 2015

The day I've been waiting for, move-in day. If I'm honest, I've been waiting for this day since the first day of high school. No joke. But the experience of leaving home was not at all how I expected. I didn't expect saying goodbye to be so hard, even though I live only an hour away. I didn't expect to miss my friends and family so much. Let's not even bring up my dog. On the upside, I really love decorating, so I was excited to make my room as cute as can be. Plus, my residence quad is the bomb dot com. As of right now, I'm really happy living here.
